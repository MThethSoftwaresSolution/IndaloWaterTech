    About Us

    Let’s partner to solve water. Use our expertise in water and wastewater treatment to reduce your water usage and save.

    iNdalo Water Technologies (Pty) Ltd is a level 1 BBBEE, CIDB 7 ME company with qualified engineers, technicians, and administrators ready to serve you.

    iNdalo, a Xhosa and Zulu word meaning nature or environment speaks to our philosophy and approach to water and wastewater. We use advanced, and environmentally friendly technologies to solve our clients’ water challenges.

    Our solutions include:

    1. Bulk chemical supply (caustic, sodium hypo, flocculants)
    2. Equipment supply (pumps, valves, and accessories)
    3. Operation & maintenance of water & wastewater plants
    4. Engineered systems such as UF/RO plants and sewage packaged plants

    No matter the industry you are in, we understand that you are constantly looking at ways to improve your water quality and reduce water usage to help your organisation save money and protect the environment. You can rely on our tried-and-tested solutions to achieve your water goals.

    Chemicals

    Most water treatment processes require chemicals to be introduced into various unit operations. This includes flocculation, sedimentation, filtration, disinfection, and pH adjustment.

    We supply our clients with these chemicals within budget, and on time, ensuring the continuous treatment of water that meets the required standards.

    • Caustic soda lye (47-50%)
    • 12-15% sodium hypochlorite
    • Polyaluminium chloride
    • Flocculants and coagulants

    O&M

    Our expert team of engineers and process controllers analyse our client’s plants and draw up an operation and maintenance programme that optimises the efficiency of the plant. Our team has assisted many municipal water and wastewater treatment works to obtain high levels of certification like the Blue Drop and Green Drop in South Africa.

    Our expert team for excellence in operation and maintenance include:

    1. Qualified and certified process engineers
    2. Effective project managers
    3. Experienced plant supervisors
    4. Certified process controllers
